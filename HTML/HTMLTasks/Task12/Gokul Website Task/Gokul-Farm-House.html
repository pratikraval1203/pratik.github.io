Gokul Farm House is a traditonal camp and farm house in Sasan Gir,covered with 200 Mango trees.
Gokul Farm House in Sasan Gir is nestling in the core of the Gir Forest which is left behind other hotels in Sasan Gir. One can take short walks into the adjoining forest.
Where our Gir Wild Life Tour Package gives you an incomparable opportunity to watch those rare Wild Asiatic Lions coupled with thousands of other elusive wild animals, it will make sure that you receive the highest standards of luxury, comfort and travel all at Gokul Farm House - Best hotel in Gir.
Take a delicious taste of Kathiyawadi pure food at Gokul.
You can enjoy gir forest & Asiatic Lion here with your family and friends, in staying our beautiful traditional tent / cottage / room. Because happiness speaks everywhere.
Gokul Farm House is located just 44kms (27.00miles) away from Somnath/Veraval, 52kms (32.00miles) away from Junagadh, 96kms (60.00miles) from Diu Airport, 157kms (97.00miles) from Rajkot Airport and about 382kms (237miles) from Ahmadabad Airport. Our farm house is easily accessible by all means.
